How to Create a New Eqp Class
  • A new equipment class could be a grouping of similar parametric items, like pumps, heat exchangers, storage tanks, etc.
  • An equipment class can be a grouping of equipment parametrics based on their discipline, or function, or vendor, or geometry (e.g. vertical vs horizontal), etc.
  • The programming aspect of defining new parametrics is covered in a different class. Existing parametric modules will be used in this lesson
