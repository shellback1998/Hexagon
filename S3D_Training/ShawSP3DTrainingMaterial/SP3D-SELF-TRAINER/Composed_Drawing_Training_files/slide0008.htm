Workflow by Snapshot
•Snapshots are on-the-fly volumes that give the user added flexibility
•Snapshot volumes are hidden by default upon creation so that there are no graphic volume boxes to deal with
•Snapshots “know” their style settings by the time they’re imported into the drawing
•We found the snapshot method to be most practical for structural framing orthos, but can be used by all other disciplines
