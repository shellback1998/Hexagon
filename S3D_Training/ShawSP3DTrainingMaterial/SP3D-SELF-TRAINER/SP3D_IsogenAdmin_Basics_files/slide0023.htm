ISOGEN Admin Basics Labs
•The following labs cover the basics of creating new styles and changing a few switches
•Iso admins with prior Isogen experience will be able to identify the new location of old familiar options switches
•Iso admins with prior Isogen experience will be able to learn about features and procedures new in SP3D
•Administrators with no prior Isogen experience may require further formal training to learn about the switches (hundreds) not included in this material
•These labs rely on extractions by pipeline. Exercises to setup extractions by piperun or spool or WBS will be added in the future, particularly WBS packages, since that’s Intergraph’s suggested method for extraction of production isos
•Go-to LABS menu and follow the instructions. Where applicable, click the movie reel icon to see a video clip of the lab completed by the instructor
