Overview
•SP3D provides a few different ways of creating orthographic drawings. Shaw procedures take advantage of the versatility of Composed Drawings
•Composed drawings are orthographic drawings created in a 3D task such as Common, Equipment, Electrical, etc.
•Composed drawings are flexible, allowing you to have views that are managed by a drawing region and associate the views to 3D volumes
•You can create a composed drawing component (like a drawing holding place) in the Drawings and Reports task, but you create the drawing documents in the 3D tasks such as Common, Equipment, Electrical, etc.
