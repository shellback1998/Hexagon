Overview
•The Electrical task provides a workspace for distributed cableway system 3D design and routing •The Electrical task uses point-by-point route design and inserts cableway and cable tray components during design
•Also, the task includes placement of conduit
•A run of tray/conduit includes straight features; along-leg features such as branches, transitions, and reducers; turn features; and end features •Placement of features is spec-dependent: Users can only place items that were previously defined in the reference data catalog •After completing cable tray design and route conduit, you then route cables through existing cable trays/conduit and define their properties. Cables are implied items and are not permanently displayed in 3D •As the cable trays are modified, cable lengths are automatically adjusted. When new cables are added to trays, the fill of the cable tray or conduit is calculated
