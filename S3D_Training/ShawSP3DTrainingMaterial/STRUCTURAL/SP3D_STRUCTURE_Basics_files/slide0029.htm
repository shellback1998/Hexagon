Members
•Linear and Curve
•Member systems: Logical collections of member parts that maintain the design basis and physical alignment of the member parts for analysis, design, and manufacturing. Connect to other member systems using Frame Connections •Member parts: represent the real, physical member parts in the model. Member parts connect logically to other member parts using Assembly Connections •The member's local X-axis is along the member axis from member start to member end •The discrete placement method requires you to define both the start and the end points of the member (good for columns) •The contiguous placement method uses the end point of the previously placed member as the start point of the next member (good for multiple beams on the same plane)
